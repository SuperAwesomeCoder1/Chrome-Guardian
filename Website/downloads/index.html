Additionally, the tutorial provided below contains additional information regarding this extension and how to use it.



Instructions
Step 1

Download the chrome extension (above or click here).

Step 2

Press 'install' in response to the prompt.

Step 3

Left click on the extension icon in the upper right-hand corner of the screen and select 'Parent Login'.

Step 4

Follow the setup prompts.

Step 5

Enjoy! For additional reference on how to use the extension, consult the following powerpoint: https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1SC8OgFysWdFyVrzdJb0Q2h2oOa-9k9j3oUW13X9sY7E.
